About Us

The team behind Luto — passionate about preserving Filipino culinary heritage one recipe at a time.

Our Mission

Luto was born out of a shared love for Filipino food and a desire to keep our culinary traditions alive. We believe that every recipe is a story — of family, of history, of home. Our goal is to make authentic Filipino cooking accessible to everyone, wherever they are in the world.
